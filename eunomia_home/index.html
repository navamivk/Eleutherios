What do we do?

At Eunomia, we aspire in making cities smarter, and save the environment while doing it. We help you locate smart bins around you to dispose your trash. Additionally, through Eunomia, your efforts will be rewarded! Based on the amount and type of trash you dispose, you can earn ether (ETH). We hope that our enterprise would provide an incentive for citizens to actively participate in waste management, and work for the betterment of our community.
